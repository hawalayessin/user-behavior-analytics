Project 1: User Behavior Analytics &
Insights Platform
1. Purpose & Vision
This project aims to build a centralized analytics and dashboard platform that provides clear,
actionable insights into how users interact with our digital services (SMS-based services,
subscriptions, trials, campaigns, etc.).
The core objective is not just reporting numbers, but understanding user behavior,
identifying drop-off reasons, measuring campaign impact, and enabling better business and
product decisions.
This project will be used by:
● Product & marketing teams to improve services and campaigns
● Technical teams to detect friction points
● Management to track performance and growth
It is intentionally designed to be suitable for:
● A data analyst (data modeling, KPIs, insights)
● Light AI usage (prediction, clustering, anomaly detection)
● Some development work (dashboard, APIs, data pipelines)
2. Business Questions the Project Must Answer
The platform should answer questions such as:
2.1 User Activity & Engagement
● How many users are currently active per service?
● How many users are inactive, and since when?
● Daily / weekly / monthly active users (DAU, WAU, MAU)
● Average lifetime of a user per service
2.2 Free Trial Behavior

=== PAGE 2 ===
● How many users start a free trial?
● How many convert to paid subscription?
● At which day do users usually drop (Day 1, Day 2, Day 3)?
● Common behavior patterns of users who stop after the free trial
2.3 Subscription & Revenue Flow (High-level)
● New subscriptions per day/week/month
● Churn rate (users who unsubscribe)
● Retention rate (Day 7, Day 14, Day 30)
● Re-subscription behavior (users who come back later)
2.4 Campaign Impact (SMS Bulk Campaigns)
● After an SMS campaign:
○ How many new users subscribed?
○ How many are still active after X days?
○ Conversion rate per campaign
● Compare campaigns against each other
● Identify the most effective campaign patterns
2.5 Cross-Service Behavior
● Do users subscribe to multiple services?
● Which services are commonly used together?
● Migration paths (Service A → Service B)
3. Service Business Model (Important Context)
All services follow the same business model, which must be clearly understood and reflected
in the analysis:
● Free trial → Automatic renewal
● Free trial duration:
○ Default: 3 days for all services
○ Can be extended temporarily during special events or promotions
● After the free trial ends:
○ The user is charged automatically based on the service plan
○ Billing cycle can be daily or weekly, depending on the service
● Fixed price per service (no dynamic pricing)
● Users can unsubscribe:
○ During the free trial

=== PAGE 3 ===
○ After the free trial
● A user cannot receive another free trial for the same service once the trial period has
ended
This business logic must be respected when calculating KPIs, churn, retention, and conversion
rates.
4. Data Sources (Initial Scope)
The analyst should expect to work with data such as:
● Users table (user_id, phone, creation date, status)
● Services table (service_id, name, billing type, price)
● Subscriptions (start_date, end_date, trial_flag)
● Unsubscription events (date, reason if available)
● SMS events (MO, MT, delivery status)
● Campaign logs (campaign_id, send_date, target size)
● Billing / charging events (success, failure, retries)
The intern is not required to design production databases, but must understand
relations, constraints, and propose improvements.
4. Key Metrics & KPIs
The dashboard should clearly expose:
● Active users per service
● Trial → Paid conversion rate
● Drop-off rate during free trial
● Retention curve (cohort-based)
● Churn rate
● Campaign ROI (subscriptions / messages sent)
● Average user lifetime
Optional advanced KPIs:
● User engagement score
● Service health score
